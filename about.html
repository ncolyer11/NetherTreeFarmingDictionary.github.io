About



A fork of https://storagetechdictionary.github.io/ and https://JoakimThorsen.github.io/MCPropertyEncyclopedia, used as a dictionary of commonly used terms, abbreviations, and expressions related to Nether Tree Farming and closely linked fields, and TMC, curated by contributors from the Huge Fungi Huggers server 


You can check out the Github Page  for the source code, the raw data, and info about contributing to this project.
